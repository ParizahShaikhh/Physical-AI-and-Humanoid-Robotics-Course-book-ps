# Quickstart: Module 5 - Deployment, Integration, and Real-World Humanoids

## Overview
Module 5 focuses on bridging simulation and AI planning into deployable humanoid systems, addressing system integration, testing, safety, and performance in real environments. This module builds on your experience with ROS 2, simulation, and VLA systems to advance toward real-world humanoid deployment.

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Modules 1-4 (ROS 2, simulation, Isaac, and VLA systems)
- Experience with ROS 2 and simulation environments
- Understanding of AI planning and perception systems
- Familiarity with safety protocols and system integration concepts

### 2. Progress Through Chapters Sequentially
The module is designed to be completed in order:
1. [From Simulation to Reality](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-1-simulation-reality.md) - Understanding the transition from simulated to real-world operation
2. [Sim-to-Real Transfer Principles](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-2-sim-to-real-transfer.md) - Key techniques for bridging simulation and reality
3. [System Integration and Runtime Orchestration](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-3-system-integration.md) - Unifying perception, planning, and control systems
4. [Safety, Constraints, and Human Interaction](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-4-safety-human-interaction.md) - Critical safety protocols for real-world deployment
5. [Testing, Debugging, and Validation](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-5-testing-validation.md) - Comprehensive validation frameworks for deployed systems
6. [Performance Evaluation and Optimization](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-6-performance-evaluation.md) - Measuring and improving real-world performance
7. [Deploying and Maintaining Autonomous Humanoids](./book-frontend/docs/module-5-real-world-humanoids/chapter-7-deployment-maintenance.md) - Complete deployment and maintenance operations

### 3. Key Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Understand and address sim-to-real transfer challenges in humanoid robotics
- Integrate perception, planning, and control into unified runtime systems
- Implement safety protocols and human interaction constraints
- Develop comprehensive testing and validation frameworks
- Evaluate and optimize humanoid performance in real-world scenarios
- Deploy and maintain autonomous humanoid systems in operational environments
