## Overview
Module 3 focuses on NVIDIA Isaac technologies for building the AI "brain" of humanoid robots, combining photorealistic simulation, synthetic data, and accelerated perception and navigation. This module builds on your experience with ROS 2 and simulation to advance toward AI-driven perception and navigation.

## Prerequisites
- Completion of Modules 1 and 2 (ROS 2 fundamentals and simulation concepts)
- Experience with ROS 2 and simulation environments
- Understanding of basic robotics concepts
- Familiarity with AI and machine learning concepts is helpful

### 3. Key Learning Objectives
By the end of this module, students will be able to:
- Explain NVIDIA Isaac Sim and its role in AI training
- Use photorealistic simulation for synthetic data generation
- Explain Isaac ROS and hardware-accelerated perception
- Understand VSLAM and navigation for humanoid robots
- Conceptually integrate AI perception with ROS 2 control
